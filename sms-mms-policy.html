SMS/MMS Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

Serenity Stays offers an SMS/MMS text messaging program for guests and subscribers who opt in to receive updates from us.

Types of Messages

Messages may include informational updates about your stay as well as recurring marketing messages about availability, seasonal offers, and promotions (for example, MMS photo updates about the property).

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. Marketing messages are sent up to approximately once per month; informational messages depend on your inquiry or stay.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply, as charged by your mobile carrier.

Consent

Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of any purchase or booking. You may opt in or out at any time.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe at any time. You will receive one final confirmation message that you have been unsubscribed, and no further messages will be sent.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact Serenity Stays through our booking platform listing.

Carrier Disclaimer

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Your mobile number and opt-in status are not shared with third parties for their marketing purposes. See our Privacy Policy for more detail.
